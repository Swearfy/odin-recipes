Pierogi

Origin

Pierogi are filled dumplings made by wrapping unleavened dough around a savoury or sweet filling and cooking in boiling water. They are often pan-fried before serving. Pierogi or their varieties are associated with the cuisines of Central, Eastern and Southeastern Europe.

Restores:

Hunger 37.5, Health 40, Sanity 5

Pros:

  • 40 HP recovery.
  • Spoil-time is 20 days.

Cons:

More stingy with the necessary ingredients: needs a Birdcage, meat, and a vegetable to use.Ideally made using smaller vegetables such as Carrots, Mushrooms, or Cactus Flesh. Farms don't usually produce small vegetables.

Using it best:

These can be used for stockpiling healing items for long trips, and a backup meal if you run out of other food somehow. If 15 days spoil-time is sufficient, Dragonpie might be a better choice, if available.

Ingredients

  • 1 Meat
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 vegetable
  • 1 Fill

Steps

  1. Add any meat in the crockpot
  2. Add the Egg in the crockpot
  3. Add any vegetable in the crockpot
  4. Add any filler you want
  5. Enjoy!
